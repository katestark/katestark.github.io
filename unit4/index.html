Think of us as your favorite neighborhood coffee shop, but with beer instead of espresso.

Stop in with co-workers to brainstorm over a pint— because we know that's when the best ideas are born— catch up with a friend, or snuggle up next to our fireplace with a good book and great brew on one of our comfy couches.

Our name comes from a term for Prohibition, "the Noble Experiment," as coined by President Herbert Hoover in 1928. However, our intentions are a little less pure.
